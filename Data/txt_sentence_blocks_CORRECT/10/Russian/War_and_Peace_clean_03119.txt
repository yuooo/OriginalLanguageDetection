`` Told whom ? '' 
`` Well , you know whom , '' said Pierre , with a meaning glance from under his brows . 
`` Prince Theodore and all those . 
To encourage culture and philanthropy is all very well of course . 
The aim is excellent but in the present circumstances something else is needed . '' 
At that moment Nicholas noticed the presence of his nephew . 
His face darkened and he went up to the boy . 
`` Why are you here ? '' 
`` Why ? 
Let him be , '' said Pierre , taking Nicholas by the arm and continuing .
