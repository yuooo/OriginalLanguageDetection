She forgot all fear of her father , went up to him , took his hand , and drawing him down put her arm round his thin , scraggy neck . 
`` Father , '' she said , `` do not turn away from me , let us weep together . '' 
`` Scoundrels ! 
Blackguards ! '' 
shrieked the old man , turning his face away from her . 
`` Destroying the army , destroying the men ! 
And why ? 
Go , go and tell Lise . '' 
The princess sank helplessly into an armchair beside her father and wept . 
She saw her brother now as he had been at the moment when he took leave of her and of Lise , his look tender yet proud .
