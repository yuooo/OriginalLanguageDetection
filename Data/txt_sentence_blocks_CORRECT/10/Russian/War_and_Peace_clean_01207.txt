Though he usually spoke easily and well , he felt a difficulty in expressing himself now while talking with Speranski . 
He was too much absorbed in observing the famous man 's personality . 
`` Grounds of personal ambition maybe , '' Speranski put in quietly . 
`` And of state interest to some extent , '' said Prince Andrew . 
`` What do you mean ? '' 
asked Speranski quietly , lowering his eyes . 
`` I am an admirer of Montesquieu , '' replied Prince Andrew , `` and his idea that le principe des monarchies est l'honneur me parait incontestable . 
Certains droits et privileges de la noblesse me paraissent etre des moyens de soutenir ce sentiment . ''
