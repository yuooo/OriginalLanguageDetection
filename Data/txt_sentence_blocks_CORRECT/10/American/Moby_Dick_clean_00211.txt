The Pipe When Stubb had departed , Ahab stood for a while leaning over the bulwarks ; and then , as had been usual with him of late , calling a sailor of the watch , he sent him below for his ivory stool , and also his pipe . 
Lighting the pipe at the binnacle lamp and planting the stool on the weather side of the deck , he sat and smoked .
