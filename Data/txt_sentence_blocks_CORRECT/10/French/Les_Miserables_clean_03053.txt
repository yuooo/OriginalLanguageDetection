Jean Valjean began to comprehend . 
Thenardier took him for an assassin . 
Thenardier resumed : `` Listen , comrade . 
You did n't kill that man without looking to see what he had in his pockets . 
Give me my half .

And half drawing from beneath his tattered blouse a huge key , he added : `` Do you want to see how a key to liberty is made ? 
Look here . '' 
Jean Valjean `` remained stupid '' -- the expression belongs to the elder Corneille -- to such a degree that he doubted whether what he beheld was real .
