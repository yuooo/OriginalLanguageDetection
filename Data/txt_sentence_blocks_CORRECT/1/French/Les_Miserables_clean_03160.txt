AT BOMBARDA 'S The Russian mountains having been exhausted , they began to think about dinner ; and the radiant party of eight , somewhat weary at last , became stranded in Bombarda 's public house , a branch establishment which had been set up in the Champs-Elysees by that famous restaurant-keeper , Bombarda , whose sign could then be seen in the Rue de Rivoli , near Delorme Alley .
