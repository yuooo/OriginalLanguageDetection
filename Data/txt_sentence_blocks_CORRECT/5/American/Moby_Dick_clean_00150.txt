The sailors mark him ; more and more certain grow their suspicions of him , and at last , fully to test the truth , by referring the whole matter to high Heaven , they all-outward to casting lots , to see for whose cause this great tempest was upon them . 
The lot is Jonah 's ; that discovered , then how furiously they mob him with their questions .
