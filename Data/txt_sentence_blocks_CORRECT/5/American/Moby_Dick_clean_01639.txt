The Needle Next morning the not-yet-subsided sea rolled in long slow billows of mighty bulk , and striving in the Pequod 's gurgling track , pushed her on like giants ' palms outspread . 
The strong unstaggering breeze abounded so , that sky and air seemed vast outbellying sails ; the whole world boomed before the wind .
