A third asserts that the cause of its movement lies in the smoke which the wind carries away . 
The peasant is irrefutable . 
He has devised a complete explanation . 
To refute him someone would have to prove to him that there is no devil , or another peasant would have to explain to him that it is not the devil but a German , who moves the locomotive . 
Only then , as a result of the contradiction , will they see that they are both wrong .
