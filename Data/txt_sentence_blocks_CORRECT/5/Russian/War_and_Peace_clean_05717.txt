The group of prisoners had melted away most of all . 
Of the three hundred and thirty men who had set out from Moscow fewer than a hundred now remained . 
The prisoners were more burdensome to the escort than even the cavalry saddles or Junot 's baggage . 
They understood that the saddles and Junot 's spoon might be of some use , but that cold and hungry soldiers should have to stand and guard equally cold and hungry Russians who froze and lagged behind on the road -LRB- in which case the order was to shoot them -RRB- was not merely incomprehensible but revolting . 
And the escort , as if afraid , in the grievous condition they themselves were in , of giving way to the pity they felt for the prisoners and so rendering their own plight still worse , treated them with particular moroseness and severity .
