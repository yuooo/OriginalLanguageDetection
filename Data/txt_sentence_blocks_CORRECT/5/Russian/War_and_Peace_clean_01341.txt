'' For more than a week preparations were being made , rough drafts of letters to Nicholas from all the household were written and copied out , while under the supervision of the countess and the solicitude of the count , money and all things necessary for the uniform and equipment of the newly commissioned officer were collected . 
Anna Mikhaylovna , practical woman that she was , had even managed by favor with army authorities to secure advantageous means of communication for herself and her son . 
She had opportunities of sending her letters to the Grand Duke Constantine Pavlovich , who commanded the Guards .
