But well as he knew it , Pierre now listened to that tale as to something new , and the quiet rapture Karataev evidently felt as he told it communicated itself also to Pierre . 
The story was of an old merchant who lived a good and God-fearing life with his family , and who went once to the Nizhni fair with a companion -- a rich merchant . 
Having put up at an inn they both went to sleep , and next morning his companion was found robbed and with his throat cut . 
A bloodstained knife was found under the old merchant 's pillow . 
He was tried , knouted , and his nostrils having been torn off , `` all in due form '' as Karataev put it , he was sent to hard labor in Siberia .
