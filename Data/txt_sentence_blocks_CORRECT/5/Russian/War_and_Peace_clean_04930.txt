Again the officer turned to Gerasim and asked him to show him the rooms in the house . 
`` Master , not here -- do n't understand ... me , you ... '' said Gerasim , trying to render his words more comprehensible by contorting them . 
Still smiling , the French officer spread out his hands before Gerasim 's nose , intimating that he did not understand him either , and moved , limping , to the door at which Pierre was standing . 
Pierre wished to go away and conceal himself , but at that moment he saw Makar Alexeevich appearing at the open kitchen door with the pistol in his hand . 
With a madman 's cunning , Makar Alexeevich eyed the Frenchman , raised his pistol , and took aim .
