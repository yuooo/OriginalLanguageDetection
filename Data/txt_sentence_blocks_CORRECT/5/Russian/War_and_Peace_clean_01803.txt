He felt the fascination and delight of frenzy . 
He flung down the slab , broke it , and swooping down on her with outstretched hands shouted , `` Get out ! '' 
in such a terrible voice that the whole house heard it with horror . 
God knows what he would have done at that moment had Helene not fled from the room . 
A week later Pierre gave his wife full power to control all his estates in Great Russia , which formed the larger part of his property , and left for Petersburg alone .
