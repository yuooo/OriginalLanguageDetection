`` It 's he ! 
It 's really he ! 
He has come ! '' 
she said to herself , and rushing at him embraced him , pressed his head to her breast , and then pushed him back and gazed at his ruddy , happy face , covered with hoarfrost . 
`` Yes , it is he , happy and contented ... '' Then all at once she remembered the tortures of suspense she had experienced for the last fortnight , and the joy that had lit up her face vanished ; she frowned and overwhelmed Pierre with a torrent of reproaches and angry words .
