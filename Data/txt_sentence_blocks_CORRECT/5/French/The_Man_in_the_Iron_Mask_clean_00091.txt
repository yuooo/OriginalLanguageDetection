You who come and relate all this ; you , who rouse in my soul curiosity , hatred , ambition , and , perhaps , even the thirst of vengeance ; except you , monsieur , who , if you are the man to whom I expect , whom the note I have received applies to , whom , in short , Heaven ought to send me , must possess about you - '' `` What ? '' 
asked Aramis . 
`` A portrait of the king , Louis XIV. , who at this moment reigns upon the throne of France . '' 
`` Here is the portrait , '' replied the bishop , handing the prisoner a miniature in enamel , on which Louis was depicted life-like , with a handsome , lofty mien . 
The prisoner eagerly seized the portrait , and gazed at it with devouring eyes .
