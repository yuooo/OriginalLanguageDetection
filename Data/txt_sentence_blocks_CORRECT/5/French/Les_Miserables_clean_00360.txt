The Bishop fixed a tranquil eye on the man . 
As he opened his mouth , doubtless to ask the new-comer what he desired , the man rested both hands on his staff , directed his gaze at the old man and the two women , and without waiting for the Bishop to speak , he said , in a loud voice : -- `` See here . 
My name is Jean Valjean . 
I am a convict from the galleys . 
I have passed nineteen years in the galleys .
