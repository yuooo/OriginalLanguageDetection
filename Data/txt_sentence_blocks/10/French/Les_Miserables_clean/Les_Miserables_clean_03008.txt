should he arrive at the inextricable and the impassable ? 
would Marius die there of hemorrhage and he of hunger ? 
should they end by both getting lost , and by furnishing two skeletons in a nook of that night ? 
He did not know . 
He put all these questions to himself without replying to them . 
The intestines of Paris form a precipice . 
Like the prophet , he was in the belly of the monster . 
All at once , he had a surprise . 
At the most unforeseen moment , and without having ceased to walk in a straight line , he perceived that he was no longer ascending ; the water of the rivulet was beating against his heels , instead of meeting him at his toes . 
The sewer was now descending .
