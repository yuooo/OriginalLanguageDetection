`` How gloomy my life has become ! '' 
he said to himself . 
`` Young girls are always appearing to me , only formerly they were angels and now they are ghouls . '' 
QUADRIFRONS That evening , as he was undressing preparatory to going to bed , his hand came in contact , in the pocket of his coat , with the packet which he had picked up on the boulevard . 
He had forgotten it . 
He thought that it would be well to open it , and that this package might possibly contain the address of the young girls , if it really belonged to them , and , in any case , the information necessary to a restitution to the person who had lost it . 
He opened the envelope . 
It was not sealed and contained four letters , also unsealed . 
They bore addresses . 
All four exhaled a horrible odor of tobacco .
