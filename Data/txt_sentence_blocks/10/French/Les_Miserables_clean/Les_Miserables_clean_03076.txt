They entered the carriage once more , and the coachman mounted his box . 
`` Inspector Javert , '' said Jean , `` grant me yet another favor . '' 
`` What is it ? '' 
demanded Javert roughly . 
`` Let me go home for one instant . 
Then you shall do whatever you like with me . '' 
Javert remained silent for a few moments , with his chin drawn back into the collar of his great-coat , then he lowered the glass and front : `` Driver , '' said he , `` Rue de l'Homme Arme , No. 7 . '' 
CONCUSSION IN THE ABSOLUTE They did not open their lips again during the whole space of their ride . 
What did Jean Valjean want ? 
To finish what he had begun ; to warn Cosette , to tell her where Marius was , to give her , possibly , some other useful information , to take , if he could , certain final measures .
