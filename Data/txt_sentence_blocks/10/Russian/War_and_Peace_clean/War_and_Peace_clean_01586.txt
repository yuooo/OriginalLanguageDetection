Only after she had reached home was Natasha able clearly to think over what had happened to her , and suddenly remembering Prince Andrew she was horrified , and at tea to which all had sat down after the opera , she gave a loud exclamation , flushed , and ran out of the room . 
`` O God ! 
I am lost ! '' 
she said to herself . 
`` How could I let him ? '' 
She sat for a long time hiding her flushed face in her hands trying to realize what had happened to her , but was unable either to understand what had happened or what she felt . 
Everything seemed dark , obscure , and terrible . 
There in that enormous , illuminated theater where the bare-legged Duport , in a tinsel-decorated jacket , jumped about to the music on wet boards , and young girls and old men , and the nearly naked Helene with her proud , calm smile , rapturously cried `` bravo ! '' 
-- there in the presence of that Helene it had all seemed clear and simple ; but now , alone by herself , it was incomprehensible .
