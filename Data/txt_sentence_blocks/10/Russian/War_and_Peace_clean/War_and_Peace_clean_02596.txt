`` Yes , prayer can move mountains , but one must have faith and not pray as Natasha and I used to as children , that the snow might turn into sugar -- and then run out into the yard to see whether it had done so . 
No , but I am not praying for trifles now , '' he thought as he put his pipe down in a corner , and folding his hands placed himself before the icon . 
Softened by memories of Princess Mary he began to pray as he had not done for a long time . 
Tears were in his eyes and in his throat when the door opened and Lavrushka came in with some papers . 
`` Blockhead ! 
Why do you come in without being called ? '' 
cried Nicholas , quickly changing his attitude . 
`` From the governor , '' said Lavrushka in a sleepy voice .
