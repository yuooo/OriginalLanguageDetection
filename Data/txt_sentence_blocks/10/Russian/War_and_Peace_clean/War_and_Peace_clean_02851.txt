He turned to glance at him . 
`` I ask one thing of you , '' he said sternly , `` to obey me and not shove yourself forward anywhere . '' 
He did not say another word to Petya but rode in silence all the way . 
When they had come to the edge of the forest it was noticeably growing light over the field . 
Denisov talked in whispers with the esaul and the Cossacks rode past Petya and Denisov . 
When they had all ridden by , Denisov touched his horse and rode down the hill . 
Slipping onto their haunches and sliding , the horses descended with their riders into the ravine . 
Petya rode beside Denisov , the pulsation of his body constantly increasing . 
It was getting lighter and lighter , but the mist still hid distant objects . 
Having reached the valley , Denisov looked back and nodded to a Cossack beside him .
