Katerina Ivanovna was called to the witness-box . 
As she entered something extraordinary happened in the court . 
The ladies clutched their lorgnettes and opera-glasses . 
There was a stir among the men : some stood up to get a better view . 
Everybody alleged afterwards that Mitya had turned `` white as a sheet '' on her entrance . 
All in black , she advanced modestly , almost timidly . 
It was impossible to tell from her face that she was agitated ; but there was a resolute gleam in her dark and gloomy eyes .
