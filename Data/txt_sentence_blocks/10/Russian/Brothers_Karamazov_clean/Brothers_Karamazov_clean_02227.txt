He had no feelings for his duties as a father . 
He ridiculed those duties . 
He left his little children to the servants , and was glad to be rid of them , forgot about them completely . 
The old man 's maxim was Apres moi le deluge . 
* He was an example of everything that is opposed to civic duty , of the most complete and malignant individualism . 
` The world may burn for aught I care , so long as I am all right , ' and he was all right ; he was content , he was eager to go on living in the same way for another twenty or thirty years . 
He swindled his own son and spent his money , his maternal inheritance , on trying to get his mistress from him .
