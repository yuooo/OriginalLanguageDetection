Alyosha recalled what he had just said . 
`` I seem to be sleeping awake ... I walk , I speak , I see , but I am asleep . '' 
It seemed to be just like that now . 
Alyosha did not leave him . 
The thought passed through his mind to run for a doctor , but he was afraid to leave his brother alone : there was no one to whom he could leave him .
