The suspicion that she was there , with him , with his rival , in his arms , and perhaps laughing at him at that moment - took his breath away . 
And it was not mere suspicion , the deception was open , obvious . 
She must be there , in that lighted room , she must be behind the screen ; and the unhappy man would have us believe that he stole up to the window , peeped respectfully in , and discreetly withdrew , for fear something terrible and immoral should happen . 
And he tries to persuade us of that , us , who understand his character , who know his state of mind at the moment , and that he knew the signals by which he could at once enter the house . ''
