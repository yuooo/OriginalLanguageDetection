Countess Mary well knew that mood of his , and when she herself was in a good frame of mind quietly waited till he had had his soup and then began to talk to him and make him admit that there was no cause for his ill-humor . 
But today she quite forgot that and was hurt that he should be angry with her without any reason , and she felt unhappy . 
She asked him where he had been . 
He replied . 
She again inquired whether everything was going well on the farm .
