Pierre , answering Natasha 's words , told her how intolerable it had been for him to meet ladies at dinners and balls in Petersburg . 
`` I have quite lost the knack of talking to ladies , '' he said . 
`` It was simply dull . 
Besides , I was very busy . '' 
Natasha looked intently at him and went on : `` Mary is so splendid , '' she said .
