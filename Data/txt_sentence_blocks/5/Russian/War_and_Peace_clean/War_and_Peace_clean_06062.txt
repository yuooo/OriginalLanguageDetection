The brilliant qualities of the soldiers of the army sent to Italy , his opponents ' reluctance to fight , and his own childish audacity and self-confidence secure him military fame . 
Innumerable so called chances accompany him everywhere . 
The disfavor into which he falls with the rulers of France turns to his advantage . 
His attempts to avoid his predestined path are unsuccessful : he is not received into the Russian service , and the appointment he seeks in Turkey comes to nothing . 
During the war in Italy he is several times on the verge of destruction and each time is saved in an unexpected manner .
