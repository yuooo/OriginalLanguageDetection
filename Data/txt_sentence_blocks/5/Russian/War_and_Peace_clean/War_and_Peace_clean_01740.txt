When the voices subsided , the footmen cleared away the broken glass and everybody sat down again , smiling at the noise they had made and exchanging remarks . 
The old count rose once more , glanced at a note lying beside his plate , and proposed a toast , `` To the health of the hero of our last campaign , Prince Peter Ivanovich Bagration ! '' 
and again his blue eyes grew moist .
