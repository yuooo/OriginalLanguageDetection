He looked around , and in the direct , respectful , wondering gaze fixed upon him he read sympathy with what he had said . 
His face grew brighter and brighter with an old man 's mild smile , which drew the corners of his lips and eyes into a cluster of wrinkles . 
He ceased speaking and bowed his head as if in perplexity . 
`` But after all who asked them here ? 
Serves them right , the bloody bastards ! ''
