And it was as if they had all only waited for this to realize the significance for them of the glow they were watching . 
Sighs were heard , words of prayer , and the sobbing of the count 's old valet . 
The valet , returning to the cottage , informed the count that Moscow was burning . 
The count donned his dressing gown and went out to look . 
Sonya and Madame Schoss , who had not yet undressed , went out with him .
