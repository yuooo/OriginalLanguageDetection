Then suddenly he realized the joyful significance of that wail ; tears choked him , and leaning his elbows on the window sill be began to cry , sobbing like a child . 
The door opened . 
The doctor with his shirt sleeves tucked up , without a coat , pale and with a trembling jaw , came out of the room . 
Prince Andrew turned to him , but the doctor gave him a bewildered look and passed by without a word . 
A woman rushed out and seeing Prince Andrew stopped , hesitating on the threshold .
