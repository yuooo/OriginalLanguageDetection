Lord Byron was beginning to make his mark ; a note to a poem by Millevoye introduced him to France in these terms : a certain Lord Baron . 
David d'Angers was trying to work in marble . 
The Abbe Caron was speaking , in terms of praise , to a private gathering of seminarists in the blind alley of Feuillantines , of an unknown priest , named Felicite-Robert , who , at a latter date , became Lamennais . 
A thing which smoked and clattered on the Seine with the noise of a swimming dog went and came beneath the windows of the Tuileries , from the Pont Royal to the Pont Louis XV. ; it was a piece of mechanism which was not good for much ; a sort of plaything , the idle dream of a dream-ridden inventor ; an utopia -- a steamboat . 
The Parisians stared indifferently at this useless thing .
