When the usher , discreetly opening the door which connected the council-chamber with the court-room , bent over the back of the President 's arm-chair and handed him the paper on which was inscribed the line which we have just perused , adding : `` The gentleman desires to be present at the trial , '' the President , with a quick and deferential movement , seized a pen and wrote a few words at the bottom of the paper and returned it to the usher , saying , `` Admit him . ''
